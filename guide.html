Return, Replacement, and Refund Policy

Return & Replacement Policy

1. Eligibility for Returns:

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • Products must be unused, unworn, and in original condition, with tags attached in their original packaging.
  • Customized or personalized products are not eligible for return unless defective or damaged.

2. Process for Returns:

  • Visit our website and initiate a return request through your account.
  • Pack the product securely in its original packaging and include proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation).
  • Drop off the return package at the nearest courier facility or schedule a pickup (available in select areas).

3. Replacement Policy:

  • Products eligible for replacement include those with manufacturing defects or items damaged during transit.
  • Replacements are subject to product availability. If a replacement is not possible, a full refund will be issued.

Refund Policy

1. Refund Timeline:

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after the return is received and inspected.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. For COD orders, refunds will be processed to your bank account (details will be required).

2. Deduction:

  • If any shipping charges were incurred during the purchase, they may not be refunded unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong item shipped).

3. Non-Refundable Items:

  • Gift cards, e-gift vouchers, and discounted items are non-refundable unless defective.

4. Cancellations:

  • Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement. After this period, orders may already be processed and shipped, making them ineligible for cancellation.

For further assistance, please contact our customer support team.
